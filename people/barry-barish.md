# Barry Barish

Nobel laureate in Physics (2017) for the detection of [[gravitational waves]] via [[LIGO]]. Co-founder of the [[SAIR Foundation]]. Professor at [[UC Riverside]]. At the [[2026 Science and AI Summit]], gave opening remarks on the instrumentation gap in university hiring and a closing provocation on the unsolved problem of discovery confidence in AI-augmented science — the "five-sigma problem" for AI.
