# David Brin

Nebula Award-winning science fiction author and physicist. Advisor to NASA's NIAC program. At the [[2026 Science and AI Summit]], gave a provocation on missing contexts in AI discourse: the seven-watt brain, the AI ecosystem under evolutionary pressure, and the failure of feudalism/chaos/Skynet mental models to capture the [[Enlightenment]] model of reciprocal accountability.
