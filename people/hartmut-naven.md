# Hartmut Naven

Researcher at [[Google Quantum AI]]. At the [[2026 Science and AI Summit]], presented Google's updated quantum computing roadmap, including the modular architecture reducing physical-to-logical qubit ratio to ~52:1, [[magic state cultivation]], and two concrete examples of quantum-AI synergy: quantum echoes for molecular structure inference and quantum-generated training data.
