# Anima Anandkumar

Professor at Caltech; formerly Director of Machine Learning Research at Nvidia. Pioneer of [[neural operators]] and lead of the [[FourCastNet]] global weather model. At the [[2026 Science and AI Summit]], argued for "AI plus science" over "AI for science" and proposed [[formal verification]] via [[Lean theorem prover]] as the solution to AI-generated scientific noise.
