# RA Adhikari

Experimental physicist at Caltech working on [[LIGO]] and quantum metrology. At the [[2026 Science and AI Summit]], presented work on [[reinforcement learning]] for LIGO servo control and argued that [[co-design]] of detectors and control systems is more transformative than scaling up instrument size. Identified [[two-level systems]] in thin films as a cross-cutting bottleneck.
