# Terence Tao

Fields Medalist (2006). Professor of Mathematics at UCLA. Co-founder of the [[SIR Foundation]]. At the [[2026 Science and AI Summit]], delivered the keynote on "Mathematics Entering the 21st Century," coining the term "[[proof indigestion]]" and describing the [[distillation challenge]] and [[inverse Galois problem]] competitions.
