# Javier Duarte

Physicist working on the [[LHC]] trigger system. At the [[2026 Science and AI Summit]], described neural networks deployed on FPGAs for real-time event selection at 40 million collisions/second, and unsupervised [[anomaly detection]] at the trigger level using autoencoders in CMS and ATLAS. Creator / contributor to [[HLS4ML]].
