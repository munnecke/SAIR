# Chang-Kee Jung

Experimental physicist working on direct [[dark matter]] detection using liquid-argon time-projection chambers. At the [[2026 Science and AI Summit]], described near-sensor and in-memory computing approaches constrained by severe power budgets (~100mW) near cryogenic media, and the challenge of bringing AI compression to one of the most power-constrained environments in physics.
