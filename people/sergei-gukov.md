# Sergei Gukov

Theoretical physicist at Caltech; director of the Merkin Center; executive director of the [[American Institute of Mathematics]]. At the [[2026 Science and AI Summit]], presented on [[sparse reward]] / long horizon problems as the unifying challenge for AI in mathematical discovery, using [[Montezuma's Revenge]] and the [[Andrews conjecture]] as case studies.
