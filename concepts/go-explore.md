# Go-Explore

A reinforcement learning algorithm that succeeded on [[Montezuma's Revenge]] by explicitly addressing two failure modes identified by [[Sergei Gukov]]: detachment (pursuing a branch without returning) and derailment (losing track of the start). Used as an analogy for AI approaches to mathematical discovery with sparse reward.
