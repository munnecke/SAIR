# sparse reward

A problem setting in reinforcement learning where feedback is rare or delayed, making it hard for agents to learn. [[Sergei Gukov]] argues this is the unifying challenge of AI for scientific discovery — from fusion reactor design to mathematical conjecture. [[Go-Explore]] succeeded on [[Montezuma's Revenge]] by diagnosing two failure modes: detachment and derailment.
