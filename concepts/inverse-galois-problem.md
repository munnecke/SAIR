# inverse Galois problem

A major open problem in Galois theory: does every finite group occur as a Galois group over the rationals? The [[SIR Foundation]] framed a related challenge as an Easter egg hunt — collecting polynomials with each of 160,000 possible Galois group "colors." Run as a competition with [[LMFDB]] to generate empirical data about Galois group frequency.
