# SIR Foundation

The Science and AI Research Foundation, co-founded by [[Barry Barish]], [[Terence Tao]], and [[Chuck Ing]]. Organizer of the [[2026 Science and AI Summit]]. Runs open competitions ([[distillation challenge]], [[inverse Galois problem]]) and is building a platform for science-AI collaboration including SID (portable research identity), SIR Connect, and shared computational resources.
