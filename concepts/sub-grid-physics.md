# sub-grid physics

Physical processes in cosmological simulations that occur at scales smaller than the simulation grid (e.g., star formation, black hole accretion, supernova feedback). [[Simeon Bird]] argued that AI will increasingly replace hand-tuned sub-grid models, but this requires AI that can learn physical law rather than merely interpolate.
