# Bayesian optimization

A sequential optimization strategy that builds a probabilistic surrogate model of an objective function and uses it to select the most informative next experiment. Foundation of [[Bayesian Algorithm Execution]] ([[Daniel Ratner]]) and used by [[Scott Clark]] for multifidelity scientific workflows and by [[Javier Duarte]] for FPGA/ML co-optimization at the [[LHC]].
