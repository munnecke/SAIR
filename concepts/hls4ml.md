# HLS4ML

High-Level Synthesis for Machine Learning — a compiler framework developed at CERN (discussed by [[Javier Darte]]) that compiles neural networks to FPGA firmware for ultra-low-latency inference. Enables neural triggers at the [[LHC]] operating within one-microsecond decision windows. Has applicability beyond particle physics including neuroscience and materials science.
