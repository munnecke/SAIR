# formal verification

The use of machine-checkable proof systems (especially the [[Lean theorem prover]]) to guarantee mathematical correctness rather than relying on LLM judges or human spot-checking. Advocated by [[Alex Meiburg]] and [[Anima Anandkumar]] as an antidote to plausible-but-wrong AI-generated mathematics.
