# proof indigestion

Term coined by [[Terence Tao]] to describe the bottleneck created when AI systems generate mathematical solutions faster than existing infrastructure (journals, referees, conferences) can evaluate them. Analogous to traffic jams on roads designed for horses. See also: [[distillation challenge]], [[Lean theorem prover]].
