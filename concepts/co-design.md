# co-design

The practice of designing scientific instruments and their AI/software control systems simultaneously from inception, rather than treating software as an afterthought. Argued by [[RA Adhikari]] (LIGO), [[Daniel Ratner]] (accelerators), [[Javier Darte]] (LHC triggers), and [[Juan Tong Lee]] (dark matter) as the primary source of large AI gains in experimental physics.
