# TorchLean

A framework teased by [[Anima Anandkumar]] for formally verifying properties of neural networks using the [[Lean theorem prover]]. Represents the convergence of [[neural operators]] and [[formal verification]] — an attempt to bring machine-checkable correctness guarantees to AI-generated scientific results.
