# Andrews conjecture

A conjecture in group theory studied by [[Sergey Gukov]]'s lab as a testbed for [[sparse reward]] AI. Can be modeled as a version of Rubik's cube on an infinite, implicitly defined graph where solution paths may be super-exponentially long. Progress was blocked by a bimodal data distribution (trivially easy synthetic instances vs. mathematically interesting hard cases) until the "missing middle" was identified and filled.
