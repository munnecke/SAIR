# FourCastNet

An AI-based global weather model developed by [[Anima Anandkumar]] and colleagues at Nvidia, built on [[neural operators]]. Tens of thousands of times faster than traditional PDE solvers; produces two-week global forecasts in under a minute on a consumer GPU. Validated by ECMWF; outperforms operational models on extreme events including hurricane landfall prediction.
