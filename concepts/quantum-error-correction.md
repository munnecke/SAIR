# quantum error correction

Techniques for protecting quantum information from decoherence and other errors using redundant encoding across multiple physical qubits. [[Hartmut Neven]] reported that Google has demonstrated QEC working in practice (milestone 2), with a new modular architecture reducing the physical-to-logical qubit ratio from ~1000:1 to ~52:1.
