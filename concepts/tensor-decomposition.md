# tensor decomposition

A method for decomposing multi-dimensional arrays (tensors) into interpretable latent components — a generalization of matrix factorization to higher-order data. Used by [[Velis Papalexakis]] for [[LIGO]] glitch identification and materials science discovery. Key advantage over matrices: uniqueness of decomposition under certain conditions, enabling scientifically meaningful claims.
