# weak lensing

The subtle distortion of distant galaxy shapes by intervening mass — a key probe of dark energy and dark matter distribution. [[Kimmy Wu]] showed that neural networks applied to weak lensing maps outperform classical summary statistics, with 95% of the information gain traceable to new implicit summary statistics discovered by the model.
