# LHC

The Large Hadron Collider at CERN. [[Javier Duarte]] described its triggering challenge: 40 million collisions per second, with neural networks on FPGAs making rejection decisions in under one microsecond. Both CMS and ATLAS detectors now deploy unsupervised anomaly detection at the trigger level via autoencoders.
