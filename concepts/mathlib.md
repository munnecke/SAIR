# MathLib

The community library of formally verified mathematics for the [[Lean theorem prover]]. [[Alex Meiburg]] proposed a "MathLib for physics" as a long-term community project — a shared library of formally stated physical laws, approximations, and side conditions that would make AI-generated physics results machine-checkable.
