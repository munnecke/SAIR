# Foundation BAX

A speculative extension of [[Bayesian Algorithm Execution]] proposed by [[Daniel Ratner]], in which a large foundation model serves as the surrogate. The output of an optimization run becomes a trained physics foundation model, allowing research communities to pool simulation runs into shared models accessible to all collaborators.
