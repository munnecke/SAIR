# LIGO

The Laser Interferometer Gravitational-Wave Observatory. [[Barry Barish]] was co-awarded the 2017 Nobel Prize in Physics for its detection of gravitational waves. Appears across multiple talks: [[RA Adhikari]] on RL-based servo control, [[Velis Papalexakis]] on tensor factorization for glitch identification, [[Michael Mulligan]] in passing. Represents a paradigm of AI co-design in experimental physics.
