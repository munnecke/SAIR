# neural operators

Architectures that learn mappings between function spaces rather than fixed-resolution vectors, enabling multi-scale inputs and outputs that remain consistent as discretization is refined. Central to [[Anima Anandkumar]]'s work on [[FourCastNet]] and inverse design. Unlike standard neural networks, they converge as more data is added rather than producing artifacts.
