# gravitational waves

Ripples in spacetime produced by accelerating masses, first directly detected by [[LIGO]] in 2015. [[Barry Barish]] shared the 2017 Nobel Prize for this discovery. AI applications include RL-based servo control ([[RA Adhikari]]) and tensor factorization for noise identification ([[Velis Papalexakis]]).
