# five-sigma standard

The five-sigma (5σ) threshold is the conventional standard for claiming a discovery in particle physics. It means the probability of the observed result occurring by chance is less than 1 in 3.5 million. [[Barry Barish]] argued at the [[2026 Science and AI Summit]] that no equivalent standard yet exists for [[AI]]-augmented analysis pipelines — a central unsolved challenge for the field.
