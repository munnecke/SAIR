# Lean theorem prover

An interactive theorem prover and proof assistant in which mathematical statements and their proofs can be expressed in a formal language and mechanically verified. Advocated at the [[2026 Science and AI Summit]] by [[Alex Mberg]] and [[Anima Anandkumar]] as the key tool for filtering AI-generated mathematics. See [[TorchLean]] for neural network verification.
