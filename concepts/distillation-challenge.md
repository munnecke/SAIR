# distillation challenge

A [[SAIR Foundation]] competition launched on Pi Day 2026, built on 22 million true/false algebra problems. Contestants submit a one-page "cheat sheet" to boost weak open-source AI models from ~51% to ~80% accuracy. Described by [[Terence Tao]] as a prototype for new scientific infrastructure that can handle AI contributions at scale. See also: [[proof indigestion]].
