# two-level systems

Microscopic quantum defects in thin films (amorphous materials) that cause 1/f noise. A cross-cutting problem affecting [[LIGO]] mirror coatings, superconducting qubits, and dark matter detectors simultaneously. [[RA Adhikari]] identified eliminating TLS noise as potentially more transformative than scaling up detector size.
