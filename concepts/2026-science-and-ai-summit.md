# 2026 Science and AI Summit

A full-day workshop held at [[UC Riverside]] co-organized by the [[SAIR Foundation]] and UCR's Center for Experimental Cosmology. Featured talks by [[Barry Barish]], [[Terence Tao]], [[Anima Anandkumar]], [[David Brin]], [[Sergei Gukov]], [[Hartmut Neven]], and many others. Central themes: AI as accelerator vs. discoverer, co-design, formal verification, and the unsolved problem of discovery confidence.
