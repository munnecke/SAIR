# foundation models

Large pre-trained models that can be fine-tuned or prompted for a wide range of downstream tasks. Discussed across multiple sessions: DINO-style models for neutrino physics ([[Jianming Bian]]), [[Foundation BAX]] for experimental design ([[Daniel Ratner]]), and the open question of whether cross-experiment foundation models for physics are feasible given data-sharing barriers.
