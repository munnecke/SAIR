# magic state cultivation

A protocol developed by Google Quantum AI (reported by [[Hartmut Naven]]) for efficiently producing "magic states" needed for universal quantum computation (T-gates). Dramatically improves on prior distillation methods, contributing to the updated roadmap targeting ~100,000 physical qubits for a commercially relevant quantum computer.
