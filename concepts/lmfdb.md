# LMFDB

The L-Functions and Modular Forms Database — a major mathematical database of objects relevant to number theory and cryptography. Collaborated with [[SAIR Foundation]] and [[Terence Tao]] to design the [[inverse Galois problem]] competition, providing the mathematical infrastructure for classifying polynomial Galois groups.
